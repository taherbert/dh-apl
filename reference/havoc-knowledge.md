- **Significance:** Quick fidelity (te=0.5) noise floor: +/-0.5%. Standard (te=0.25): +/-0.25%. Confirm (te=0.1): +/-0.1%.
- **APL comments:** Plain ASCII only, no consecutive comment lines, no em dashes or decorative characters.
