# Expansion Options

Source: https://github.com/simulationcraft/simc/wiki/ExpansionOptions

Expansion-specific options control trinket behaviors, embellishments, and seasonal mechanics. These options may be removed in newer expansion versions of SimC.

## The War Within

All options are player scoped unless noted.
